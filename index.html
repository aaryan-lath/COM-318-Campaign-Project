Persuasion Strategy

This campaign uses the Elaboration Likelihood Model (ELM) to persuade members of Congress via:

  • Central Route: Budget facts, economic data, national benefit reports.
  • Peripheral Route: Public support tools, emotional appeals to patriotism.

We also apply Cognitive Dissonance Theory by showing inconsistency between legislators' public STEM support and low NASA funding.
